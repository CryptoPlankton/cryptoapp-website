It’s Thursday. You wake up to $15 Crypto App notification. A pal paid you back for that pizza you shared.

You top up your card on your on your phone with funds from your crypto box. You use it later to pay for coffee.

You send a private message to you friend having peace of mind your money and messages are your own.

Download Crypto App Store Google Play

Ingredients

  1. Android or iOS Crypto app
  2. Raspberry Pi + Crypto App installer
  3. Blank Card (two-way rfid card) (optional)
  4. Printed Raspberry Pi Case (optional)

How it works

Your coins are kept on the box under a single ‘Crypto Address’ kind of like a phone number that you control using an app from your phone.

You can give out this address and anyone can send you ANY coin via this address.
